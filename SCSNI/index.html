SCSNI-UCI

Specialty Care Safety Net Initiative @ UC Irvine

This is a temporary information page regarding the SCSNI Project at UCI. It is for internal use only. Do not share this page with external parties, including clinics and CCPH.

Important Links

Referral Receipt Form : This is a public Google Form used to input specialty requests and their clinic of origin, information needed for both Technical and Program coordinators. It does not contain patient identifiable information. The associated private Google Spreadsheet contains additional fields for post-consultation questions. If you have a Google Account please ask Hector for access to the spreadsheet.

Scheduling Calendar : This is a public Google Calendar listing specialty blocks. If you have a Google Account please ask Hector for edit access.

Informative Links

Center for Connected Health Policy (CCHP) : The California Center for Connected Health (CCCH) changed its name to the Center for Connected Health Policy (CCHP). They oversee the SCSNI project.
